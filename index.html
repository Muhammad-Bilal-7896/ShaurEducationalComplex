Knowledge Comes but Wisdom Lingers :
The name of the school has its semantic value. Allah Almighty shows his like for those who are thoughtful in their actions. So our school imparts “ Shaur ” , hikmat and consciousness to its learners. Our school has a constructive and creative approach. The school provides positive outlook to life and is a prop to educative learning. It builds the character of the students. It promotes Islamic values. The behavior of the students is given a direction according to Islamic values. The Islamic atmosphere is encouraged. It polishes the character of the students; it instigates a spirit in them to be a successful person. It makes our student disciplined. They act on the motto of “Faith, Unity and Discipline”. They are given a realization of importance of time and energy and orderly behavior. Our school prepares the students to face the new challenges of life. They get tactics and skills to be equipped for the practical life of present and the future.
